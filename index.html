Pro Tips for Best Results

Use Good Quality Photos: Clear, well-lit images with good contrast between subject and background produce the best results. Avoid blurry or low-resolution photos.

Solid Backgrounds Work Best: Images with solid or simple backgrounds (white, blue, solid colors) are processed most accurately. Complex, cluttered, or similar-colored backgrounds may require touch-ups.

Ensure Good Lighting: Well-lit subjects with clear edges process faster and more accurately than poorly lit images.

Center Your Subject: Keep your main subject centered and ensure it fills most of the frame for optimal results.

Check Hair and Details: For portraits with hair, fur, or fine details, review the result carefully. Our AI handles these well, but complex hair patterns might need minor adjustments in Photoshop if needed.

Transparency vs. White Background: When to Use Each

Transparent Backgrounds (PNG): Use transparent backgrounds when you need flexibility in how the image will be used. Transparent PNGs work great for composite designs, layering with other images, or placing on custom backgrounds. All our downloads are transparent PNGs.

White Backgrounds (JPG): While our tool produces transparent PNGs, if you need a white background instead, you can easily add one in any image editor or convert the PNG to JPG on a white background using free online tools.
